What we've done

Dominion Medical HMS

We helped to build an integrated information system designed to manage all aspects of their hospital's operation!

Wasn't too long till Dominion Medical Center in Ado-Ekiti, Nigeria realised that they needed more than just using unreliable papers and files for patients data, not to talk of balancing high-quality patient services with the evolving healthcare standards, emerging e-business trends, rising patient expectations and a demand for lower healthcare costs .

We helped Dominion Medical Center to build a user-friendly Hospital Management Web-based application that reduces the burden of managing all hospital processes including the hospital compartments and staff, patient management, billing, payment and reminder automation, appointment scheduling, and even a blog section to feed medical knowledge to patients.

Take Away Online Delivery

We helped to build an online presence for food ordering, online payment/payment on delivery, and meal deliveries!

When Take Away Eateries started to think of engaging, retaining, and keeping her customers, as well as having a larger online customer base than the current offline customers, we met them with a perfect solution to their needs.

We helped Take Away Eateries to open a reliable branch online, whose primary goal is to support online deliveries and transactions, engage current and prospective customers, and quite easy to maintain by an in-house employee.
